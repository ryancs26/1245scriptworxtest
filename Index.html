Key features

Every aspect of ScriptwoRx was designed to mirror the way specialists prescribe: fast, accurate, and always compliant with local regulatory requirements.

Designed for specialists

Built for medical specialists who prescribe complex authority medications so you can create, review, and send scripts without unnecessary GP-focused steps.

Authority-ready workflows

Structure scripts for Highly Specialised Drugs with all PBS requirements captured up front, reducing back-and-forth for authority approvals.

Secure digital delivery

Encrypted, cloud-based prescribing that keeps patient data safe while giving your team access from any secure, compliant workstation.

Regulatory alignment

ScriptwoRx aligns with Australian Digital Health Agency initiatives and integrates with national Health Identifier infrastructure.
